Santiago Bernabéu Stadium (Spanish: Estadio Santiago Bernabéu, [esˈtaðjo sanˈtjaɣo βeɾnaˈβew]) is a football stadium in Madrid, Spain. With a seating capacity of 80,000, the stadium has the second-largest seating capacity for a football stadium in Spain.
